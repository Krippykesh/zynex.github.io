Meet ZYNEX

ZYNEX is a student-led startup founded by the Computer Engineering Department of SSM Polytechnic College, Tirur. As passionate learners and innovators, we aim to transform ideas into impactful solutions through creativity, technology, and collaboration.

We empower small businesses, entrepreneurs, and organizations with a wide range of services including web and app development, digital solutions, social media marketing, content creation, market analytics, and brand identity building. In addition, we also provide traditional marketing, advertising, and other offline services to ensure complete support for every client.

At ZYNEX, we focus on understanding your unique vision to design strategies that truly reflect your goals. Whether you are building your business from the ground up or looking to refresh your image, we can help you craft a strong identity, identify your target audience, and launch campaigns that deliver real results.

Being students of Computer Engineering, we bring fresh perspectives, technical expertise, and enthusiasm to every project. ZYNEX is not just a startup—it’s a platform where young minds innovate, inspire, and create change.
